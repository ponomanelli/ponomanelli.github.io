Welcome to MUCFD

MUCFD is an organization for students that are passionate about fashion and design.
Our goals are to provide a program that will offer students interested in fashion a chance to further their artistry, ideas and talent in order to succeed and gain experience in the Industry. Working in conjunction with Miami University and the community, we hope to build a legacy and foundation for students for years to come.
